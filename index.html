    Here's a Timeline of Vlad the Impaler:

  • 1428 - The song of the dragon was born
  • 1448 - Vlad was old enough to claim the throne of Wallachia.
  • 1456 - Earned his moniker by impaling Saxons after raiding their village and capturing them.
  • 1460 - Impaled Sultan Mehmed's two envoys
  • 1462- Vlad was imprisoned by King Corvinus of Hungary
  • 1475 - Vlad was killed in battle near Bucharest after returning to conquer Wallachia.
  • 1897 - Turned into a vampire by Bram Stoker
  • 2005 - Confirmed to be a vampire by Elizabeth Kostova in her non-fiction work The Historian
  • 2014 - Starred in his own movie using the stage name "Luke Evans"
